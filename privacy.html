Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

Colors Math is a game focused on mental math and color mixing challenges. This privacy policy explains how the app handles data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The app does not require accounts and does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

Data Stored on Your Device

Colors Math stores basic game data locally on your device, such as your high score and your settings (sound, vibration, language, and difficulty). This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Colors Math does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app. When we do, we will update the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer at contact@example.com.
